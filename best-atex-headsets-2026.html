5 Best ATEX Headsets for Hazardous Areas (2026)

Last updated: June 2026

Choosing the right intrinsically safe headset depends on your zone classification, communication needs, and budget. Here are our top 5 picks for 2026, ranked by use case.

1

Sensear SM1P-Ex

Zone 1

The SM1P-Ex is the most complete headset on the market. It covers every communication method — two-way radio, Bluetooth, and face-to-face via SENS® technology — all in a Zone 1 certified package. The triple certification (ATEX + IECEx + UL913) means it works globally without separate approvals.

NRR27 dB
Battery24 hr
Price~$1,200

2

3M Peltor LiteCom PRO III Ex

Best for Zone 0 — built-in radio, no separate device needed

Zone 0

The only headset with a built-in PMR446/UHF radio certified for Zone 0. Workers can communicate directly without carrying a separate ATEX radio — reducing cost and complexity. The 3 km range covers most plant environments.

Battery14 hr

Same SENS® technology and 24-hour battery as the SM1P-Ex, but without Bluetooth — saving ~$300. If your team only needs two-way radio communication (no smartphone pairing), this is the sweet spot for Zone 1 operations.

If you need Bluetooth headset connectivity (for smartphone calls, music, or alerts) in a Zone 0 environment without two-way radio, this is the best option. The 30-hour battery means multi-shift use without charging, and the ambient listening feature keeps workers aware of their surroundings.
